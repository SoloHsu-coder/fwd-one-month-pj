What is "Wuxia" series?

Wuxia series is basically fighting in the real world with humans. Usually with inner chi etc and fighting techniques.Fictional stories about regular humans who can achieve supernatural fighting ability through Chinese martial arts training, sometimes involving internal energy use and development. Themes of chivalry, tragedy, revenge & romance are common.
